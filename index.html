Guidelines of the contest

Read Carefully!
  • The best memes will be selected on the total number of Likes, Comments and Shares the post gets to ensure transparency. So, the memes that will attract the maximum number of Likes, comments & shares will be the winner.
  • Each like gets 1 point, each comment receives 2 points, and each share gets 2 points.
  • A participating team’s total number of likes, comments and shares of all the posts will be taken as a final number on date 15.02.2022, 3 pm.
  • You can use 30 hashtags per meme. 10 default hashtags and 2 Tags mentioned by us.
  • Participants should never use or tag any name or social media handles of other companies in their posts of any sort. Such usage will disqualify you from further participating in the contest
  • The name of the winners will be announced and featured on 17th Feb 2022 on our social media platforms. A certificate and the cash prize will be distributed to the winners in 7 days.
  • The respective memes should be posted only on the respective dates of topics. And only one post should be made on a topic. Even with more impressions, any second post will not be considered for selecting the winners.
  • Any similarity in the contents with other participants will not be entertained, but the memes are posted simultaneously. We understand there can be similarities, but the exact duplication will not be counted.
  • You can post the memes from only one account (Registered Facebook and Instagram Account), and the memes directly published in other accounts will not be considered for counting.
  • Apart from the contest, the best quality content creator can get a career opportunity in the creative team of our company.
